The Opportunity

Join Our Team as a QA Experienced Tester Shape the Future of Payment Issuer Solutions!

The Core Foundation & Integration Tribe (CFI) implements and delivers the Issuing core applications, from Card Issuing lifecycle to Transactions clearing & settlement. With over 150 million cards and 1.16 billion monthly transactions, our platforms operate at the forefront of capacity and performance.

As a QA Tester, you will integrate into a diverse, agile, and collaborative cross-cultural team consisting of product owner, developers, and testers. You will play a pivotal role in ensuring the quality and reliability of all products across our financial services portfolio, working as an enabler for one or several squads. In this role, you'll work on the technical side by testing applications, automating the test cases and managing our test automation tools while building a strong understanding of the business requirements.

Day-to-Day Responsibilities

  • Analyse user stories, functional specifications, and technical requirements to create comprehensive and well-structured test plans and test cases.
  • Collaborate with Product Owners / Business Analyst / Developers to clarify requirements and acceptance criteria, ensuring testability from the outset.
  • Design, execute and document test scenarios (manual & automated) for unitary, component & integration tests, for API, GUI, interfaces & Batch software.
  • Identify, document, and track defects while working closely with development teams for resolution.
  • Actively support company quality standards creating robust automated regression suites when appropriate & using predefined processes and tools. Support the continuous improvement process and propose your own measures.
